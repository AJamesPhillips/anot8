Anot8 was built to work as a local server for annotating your PDFs, however it now also supports loading any PDF by a URL and searching Unpaywall.org by DOI for free copies of articles.

"Temporary" annotations

It also allows you to add, edit and delete "temporary" annotations that are only stored in the URL of the page. This means Anot8 never stores your private data, and you have no dependence on Anot8 or our server, but can still embed links to other annotated documents in to your own documents for others to use.


Auto redirect

This allows you to use perma links like anot8.org/r/1772.1/1 in your internal documents, and have all your team access the PDFs and annotations from a local server (faster) or password protected public server (privacy).
